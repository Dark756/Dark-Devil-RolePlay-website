Discord Rules

  • 1 : Be respectful.
  • 2 : Post content in the correct channels.
  • 3 : Mentioning roles, staff, or a specific person without proper reason is prohibited.
  • 4 : Listen to what the Staff says.
  • 5 : No racism or discrimination.
  • 6 : No offensive messages or nicknames (Anything that a reasonable person might find offensive).
  • 7 : Sending/Linking any harmful material such as viruses, IP grabbers or harmware results in an immediate and permanent ban.
  • 8 : Do not spam several channels with the same post. For example, if you are promoting an event, post it in one relevant channel.
  • 9 : Usage of excessive extreme inappropriate language is prohibited.
  • 10 : No NSFW / sexually explicit content or language.
  • 11 : Spamming mass messages, mass uploading images & spamming emojis to interrupt a conversation is not allowed.
  • 12 : No advertising or poaching.
  • 13 : Do not threaten anyone.
  • 14 : No harassment of any kind.
  • 15 : If you are one of two users dominating the conversation in a channel, please consider moving your conversation to Direct Messages.
  • 16 : Don't attack for rp matters in DM.
  • 17 : Warning > 1 Hour Mute > 24 Hour Mute > 1 Week Ban > Permanent Ban
  • 18 : Depending on the severity of the breach, staff members are at liberty to deviate from this system

## SERVER RULES ##

server Rule #1

# Green Zones

  • 1 : Garages.
  • 2 : Vehicle Shop
  • 3 : Hospitals. You can't Harm, Shoot, Carry, Take-Hostage someone in Green Zone. Park your Cars in Right manner & Keepout from using Horn/Burnout.

# Blue Zone

  • 1 : Blue Zone includes Police Department areas where extra protection are afforded to police.

# Red Zone

  • 1 : Red Zone includes Weed Farms and Weed, Coke, Meth and Opium Factory/Processing Areas. Mostly Gang turfs where they rule on it.

# FearRP

  • 1 : FearRP - Players must value their lives. You can take revive and respawn but must value it.
  • 2 : Player also should value thier Vehicles, Items or Assets. (Example: Do not try to run or punch someone when they have a gun to your head. Comply with all demands of criminals for fear of retaliation.) RDM & VDM
  • 3 : No RDM (Random Death Match) - You must not kill someone without a valid reason or giving them a reasonable ultimatum.
  • 4 : No VDM (Vehicle Death Match) - You can't kill/Harm Someone with any given reason with a vehicle. Combat-Login
  • 5 : You can't Disconnect from a RP situation without completing it. You can't Disconnect while in a Police Chase, Police Custody, Cell & Under Observation of Police even Respawn is available

server Rule #2

  • 1 : You cant use Hate Speech and Slang.
  • 2 : You cant talk after death. You must cooperate with the given situation weather if you are at Pain, Hurt or Dead. If you are knocked unconscious or killed, you have to act as an injured person. You can't run or fight.
  • 3 : You must not Carry or do Toxicity with a dead body. You can still take it to the hospital with a good intention.
  • 4 : Participating in the same situation or location after being dead within 15 minutes is strictly prohibited.
  • 5 : Doing 3rd Party in Robbery or Code-Red is prohibited.
  • 6 : You must not share information about illegal processing with others or teach to new players. They have figure it out on their own.
  • 7 : Exploiting bugs or using hacks. Using Glitch or Emote for Taking Advantage.

# House Robbery

  • 1 : A house robbery scenario can have a maximum of 2 robbers and 2 policemen
  • 2 : CodRed is not allowed in a house robbery situation
  • 3 : The police will only have the right to look at you.
  • 4 : If you have a gun/firearm, the police have the right to use their gun/firearm in self-defense or to control the situation.

server Rule #3

  • ** Bank/Store Robbery You can claim 2 out of 4 claims **
  • 1 : No GPS.
  • 2 : No spikes.
  • 3 : Free passage.
  • 4 : 7s head start. Other Information
  • 5 : No one can escape or take shelter in the Green Zone or any gang/persona house or company.
  • 6 : A complete bank robbery scenario at Pacific Bank (the largest) may involve only 5 people and at Flica Bank 4 people and 3 people in a shop robbery incident.
  • 7 : Jumping into the ocean will result in code red. Off-roading is not permitted without an off-road vehicle
  • 8 : 2 additional units will respond to police form when code red starts.
  • 9 : If you have backup outside of the robbery situation, they should be 2 km away from the robbery location.
  • 10 : Fake hostages are not allowed. (Example: Friends pretending to be hostages.) Note: The police will not make any demands after the robbers initiate a code red.
  • 11 : Free passage as standard

server Rule #4

# Gangs and parties

  • 1 : A coded situation can involve a maximum of 4 people.
  • 2 : You may not intend to assist gang members in a Code-Red situation if you are not registered with a specific official gang.
  • 3 : Only official gangs can claim taxes in illegal areas.
